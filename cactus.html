Sarvakri

Druid, Circle of the Moon

Sarvakri cannot recall a single memory of the time before her third summer. But she can recall every single detail from the summer soltice of her third summer. The day the Moon Grove found her.

Worshippers of nature and children of the moon, they took in young Sarvakri under their wing and raised her as one of their own. Soon the druids became her family, and through them she learned of the gifts the land had bestowed onto them and swore to protect them from evil.
